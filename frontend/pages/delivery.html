Delivery Information

1. Delivery Zones & Fees
  • Inside Dhaka: 80 BDT (1–3 business days)
  • Outside Dhaka: 140 BDT (3–5 business days)

2. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with tracking details. Please ensure your name, address, and phone number are correct to avoid delivery delays.
